About KeepUp

KeepUp is a business monitoring platform created to help sellers understand how their businesses are performing.

The system focuses on clarity, simplicity, and usability, allowing sellers to make informed decisions using organised data.

This project demonstrates structured HTML, responsive design, and user-friendly layout principles.
